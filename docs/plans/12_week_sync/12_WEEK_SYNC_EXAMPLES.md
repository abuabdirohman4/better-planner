## Reflection Question Library

### Standard Questions (Always Ask)

1. **Kesulitan apa yang saya hadapi dalam proses 12 Minggu ini?**
   - What obstacles appeared?
   - What surprised me (negatively)?
   - What would I change if I could redo?

2. **Nasihat dari saya untuk 12 Minggu ke depan**
   - Based on this quarter, what wisdom can I share with future me?
   - What should I definitely repeat?
   - What should I definitely avoid?

3. **Reward Untuk Diri Saya**
   - How will I celebrate this quarter's wins?
   - What do I deserve?
   - When will I enjoy it?

4. **Goal mana yang memerlukan komitmen Anda kembali?**
   - Which goals need renewed focus?
   - What fell by the wayside?
   - What's still important but neglected?

5. **Goal mana yang perlu Anda revisi?**
   - Which goals no longer serve me?
   - What should I drop?
   - What needs significant change?

### Optional Deep-Dive Questions

6. **What am I most proud of this quarter?**
7. **What surprised me (positively) about my performance?**
8. **Which habits/systems served me best?**
9. **Which relationships improved/suffered?**
10. **How aligned was I with my 3-year vision?**
11. **What did I learn about myself?**
12. **If I could give past me (12 weeks ago) one piece of advice, what would it be?**
13. **What energy patterns did I notice? (Best weeks? Worst weeks?)**
14. **What external factors impacted my quarter?**
15. **What am I grateful for from this quarter?**

### Life Area Balance Questions

16. **Karir:** Did my career progress align with my vision?
17. **Kesehatan:** Am I healthier now than 12 weeks ago?
18. **Relasi:** Are my relationships stronger?
19. **Spiritual:** Did I grow spiritually?
20. **Keuangan:** Is my financial situation improved?
21. **Petualangan:** Did I have fun and explore?
22. **Kontribusi:** Did I make a positive impact?

## Usage Tips

### Before Starting 12 Week Sync

1. **Schedule dedicated time:** 1-2 hours, quiet space
2. **Gather data:**
   - Open High Focus Goals
   - Review all 12 Weekly Syncs
   - Check Habit Tracker (3 months)
   - Review Best Week adherence
3. **Set honest mindset:** This is learning, not judgment

### During Review

1. **Start with wins:** List accomplishments first (positive frame)
2. **Be specific:** "Unclear goals" vs "I didn't define measurable success criteria"
3. **Be honest:** It's just you - no need to impress
4. **Look for patterns:** Not just "what" but "why"
5. **Future-focused:** Learnings → actions for next quarter

### After Completing

1. **Schedule reward:** Actually put it in calendar
2. **Share key insights:** With partner/mentor/friend
3. **Set up next quarter:** Don't wait, create structure immediately
4. **Celebrate:** You just completed a significant reflection!

## Common Patterns Across Quarters

### High Achievers (8+ average)
- Clear, specific goals from day 1
- Weekly accountability rituals
- Strong systems (Best Week, habits, etc.)
- Mid-quarter adjustment when needed
- Good energy management

### Moderate Achievers (5-7 average)
- Some goals clear, others fuzzy
- Inconsistent weekly reviews
- Systems exist but not always followed
- React to problems vs prevent
- Room for optimization

### Struggling (1-4 average)
- Vague goals OR overly ambitious
- No weekly check-ins
- No supporting systems
- External chaos (legitimate!)
- OR need to simplify dramatically

**Note:** One "bad" quarter doesn't mean failure! Life happens. Learn and adjust.
