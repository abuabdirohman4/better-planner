# BEST WEEK CONCEPT - Sync Planner Framework

## Overview

**Best Week** adalah model dan acuan untuk minggu ideal yang paling produktif. Konsep ini membantu user secara proaktif menentukan bagaimana menghabiskan waktu dan menjadikannya acuan untuk bertindak setiap hari.

> "When you have a plan, you develop an unbelievable power to decide proactively and to say 'NO'." - Hyrum Smith

## Core Philosophy

Best Week bukan tentang perfeksionisme, tapi tentang **intentional living**:
- Proactive time allocation vs reactive scheduling
- Balanced life across 7 areas (Karir, Kesehatan, Relasi, Kontribusi, Petualangan, Keuangan, Spiritual)
- Sustainable productivity vs burnout
- 80% adherence = excellent (bukan 100% rigid)

## 4 LEVEL AKTIVITAS

### 1. High Lifetime Value Activity (HLVA)

**Definition:**
Aktivitas yang memberikan dampak paling panjang dalam hidup kita.

**Characteristics:**
- Long-term impact on life quality
- Personal growth & development
- Relationship building
- Health & spiritual practices
- Deep learning that transforms

**Examples:**
- Quality time dengan keluarga
- Exercise & health practices
- Spiritual development (doa, meditasi)
- Deep learning (buku, course transformative)
- Building meaningful relationships
- Skill development for career advancement

**Time Allocation:** 2-4 hours per day
**Priority:** HIGHEST - these are "big rocks"
**Mapping:** → **DAILY QUEST** (Foundation Time)

---

### 2. High Rupiah Value Activity (HRVA)

**Definition:**
Aktivitas yang menjadi alasan utama mengapa Anda menjalankan peran di pekerjaan atau bisnis. Core value-creating work.

**Characteristics:**
- Directly revenue/impact generating
- Core competency work
- Strategic vs operational
- Deep focus required
- High leverage activities

**Examples:**
- Sales calls (untuk sales role)
- Strategic planning (untuk leader)
- Core product development (untuk engineer)
- Client delivery (untuk consultant)
- Content creation (untuk creator)
- Deep work on High Focus Goals

**Time Allocation:** 4-6 hours per day
**Priority:** HIGH - focused work blocks
**Mapping:** → **MAIN QUEST / WORK QUEST** (Focused Time)
**LifeOS Alignment:** → **Focused Hours** (Deep Work)

---

### 3. Low Rupiah Value Activity (LRVA)

**Definition:**
Pekerjaan yang bersifat persiapan, necessary but not high-impact, perlu dilakukan tapi bukan core.

**Characteristics:**
- Necessary but not sufficient
- Support work vs core work
- Can be done at lower energy times
- Batchable & schedulable
- Should NOT be done at prime time

**Examples:**
- Email admin & correspondence
- Meeting koordinasi (routine)
- Filing/organizing
- Routine updates & reports
- Update finance tracking
- Light cleaning & maintenance

**Time Allocation:** 1-2 hours per day
**Priority:** MEDIUM - do at off-peak energy times
**Mapping:** → **DAILY QUEST** (maintenance) + **SIDE QUEST**

---

### 4. Zero Rupiah Value Activity (ZRVA)

**Definition:**
Kegiatan yang membawa kita "kemana-mana" - distractions and time wasters.

**Characteristics:**
- Immediate gratification, no long-term value
- Procrastination enablers
- Energy drains
- Busy work without purpose
- Should be minimized/eliminated

**Examples:**
- Social media scrolling (unintentional)
- Netflix binge (beyond rest)
- Excessive gosip/chit-chat
- Random web browsing
- Busy work tanpa purpose
- Checking phone compulsively

**Time Allocation:** MINIMIZE! Ideally <30 min/day
**Priority:** ELIMINATE or time-box strictly
**Mapping:** → **TO DON'T LIST**

### Best Week → 7 Life Areas Coverage

User's Best Week should ensure coverage of all 7 areas from Sync Planner:

1. **Karir** - High Rupiah activities + learning (KPD)

3. **Relasi** - Family time, friends, quality connections

5. **Petualangan** - Hobbies, exploration, fun
6. **Keuangan** - Finance tracking, planning, wealth building
7. **Spiritual** - Prayer, meditation, reflection, growth

```

             │ Morning Routine, Exercise, Learning
             │ → DAILY QUEST

             │ Deep Work Block 1
             │ → MAIN/WORK QUEST (Focused Hours)

12:00-13:00  │ TRANSITION
             │ Lunch + Break

13:00-17:00  │ HIGH RUPIAH VALUE (4h)
             │ Deep Work Block 2
             │ → MAIN/WORK QUEST (Focused Hours)

17:00-18:00  │ LOW RUPIAH VALUE (1h)
             │ Admin, Email, Maintenance
             │ → DAILY/SIDE QUEST

18:00-21:00  │ HIGH LIFETIME VALUE (3h)
             │ Family Time, Personal Projects
             │ → Life (not always tracked)

21:00-22:00  │ HIGH LIFETIME VALUE (1h)
             │ Evening Routine, Planning
             │ → DAILY QUEST
```

### Weekend Template (Sabtu-Minggu)

```
07:00-09:00  │ HIGH LIFETIME VALUE
             │ Relaxed morning, exercise

09:00-12:00  │ FLEXIBLE TIME
             │ Personal projects / Family / Learning

             │ Quality time, hobbies, rest

18:00-21:00  │ SOCIAL/SPIRITUAL TIME

21:00-22:00  │ PLANNING
             │ Weekly Review (Sabtu)
             │ Weekly Planning (Minggu)
```

## USER WORKFLOW

### 1. Initial Setup (One-time)

User creates their ideal Best Week:

1. **Activity Inventory**
   - List all weekly activities
   - Categorize each: HLVA, HRVA, LRVA, ZRVA

2. **Time Blocking**
   - Design ideal weekday schedule
   - Design ideal weekend schedule
   - Allocate hours to each activity level

3. **Quest Mapping**
   - Map HLVA → Daily Quest items
   - Map HRVA → Main/Work Quest focus
   - Map LRVA → Daily/Side Quest tasks
   - Map ZRVA → To Don't List

4. **Validation**
   - Check 7 life areas coverage
   - Ensure sustainability (not overcommitted)
   - Set realistic time budgets

### 2. Weekly Planning (Setiap Minggu)

User references Best Week untuk:

1. **Weekly Goal Setting**
   - Set 3 Weekly Goals aligned dengan Best Week priorities
   - Schedule High Rupiah work blocks
   - Plan High Lifetime activities

2. **Daily Quest Setup**
   - Set recurring Daily Quest items from Best Week template
   - Adjust for special circumstances this week

3. **To Don't List**
   - Identify Zero Rupiah activities to avoid
   - Set boundaries based on Best Week

### 3. Daily Execution (Setiap Hari)

Morning:
- Review Best Week → What's today's focus?
- Check Daily Quest foundation tasks
- Plan Quest sessions for High Rupiah work

Evening:
- Compare actual vs Best Week template
- Adjust tomorrow's plan
- Mastering Transition

### 4. Weekly Review (Sabtu/Minggu)

Compare Actual Week vs Best Week:
- Calculate adherence % per activity level
- Identify gaps and wins
- Adjust Best Week template if needed
- Plan next week

## KEY PRINCIPLES

### 1. Best Week ≠ Perfect Week

- It's a GUIDE, not rigid rules
- Flexibility for life circumstances
- 80% adherence is excellent
- Adjust template as life changes

### 2. "Big Rocks" First

- Schedule High Lifetime first (foundation)
- Then High Rupiah (focused work)
- Then Low Rupiah fits around them
- Zero Rupiah gets squeezed out

### 3. Protect Prime Time

- High Rupiah work during peak energy
- Low Rupiah during lower energy
- Don't waste morning on email!

### 4. Balance is Built-In

- Best Week ensures 7 areas coverage
- Not just work optimization
- Sustainable long-term approach

### 5. Proactive Power

> "When you have a plan, you develop unbelievable power to say NO"

Best Week gives you:
- Clear priorities
- Guilt-free boundaries
- Confident decisions
- Reduced decision fatigue

## TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Creating Your Best Week

1. **Start with reality** - base on actual good weeks
2. **Don't overcommit** - leave buffer time
3. **Test & iterate** - run for 2-3 weeks, then adjust
4. **Get input** - discuss with family/team if needed
5. **Align with goals** - ensure Best Week serves High Focus Goals

### Using Best Week Daily

1. **Morning check-in** - "What's my Best Week say for today?"
2. **Protect big rocks** - High Lifetime non-negotiable
3. **Guard prime time** - High Rupiah during peak energy
4. **Batch Low Rupiah** - group admin tasks
5. **Track Zero Rupiah** - awareness of time leaks

### Maintaining Best Week

1. **Weekly review** - compare actual vs template
2. **Seasonal adjust** - update for life changes
3. **Don't guilt trip** - some weeks will be different
4. **Celebrate adherence** - acknowledge good weeks
5. **Learn from variance** - why did you diverge?
