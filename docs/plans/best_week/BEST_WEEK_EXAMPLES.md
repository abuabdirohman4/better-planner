## Usage Guide

### How to Use These Templates

1. **Pick Starting Point**
   - Choose template closest to your role/situation
   - Don't try to copy exactly - use as inspiration

2. **Customize**
   - Adjust time blocks to your schedule
   - Add/remove activities based on your goals
   - Match to your energy patterns (morning person vs night owl)

3. **Validate Against 7 Areas**
   - Karir: Enough High Rupiah + learning?
   - Kesehatan: Exercise + healthy habits?
   - Relasi: Quality time blocked?
   - Kontribusi: Service/impact work?
   - Petualangan: Fun & exploration?
   - Keuangan: Finance management?
   - Spiritual: Practice & reflection?

4. **Test & Iterate**
   - Run for 1-2 weeks
   - Track adherence
   - Adjust unrealistic parts
   - Keep what works

5. **Seasonal Adjust**
   - Update quarterly
   - Account for life changes
   - Don't guilt trip - adapt!

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Too ambitious** - 12h+ tracked time daily
✅ **Sustainable** - 8-10h tracked, rest is life

❌ **No buffer** - Every minute scheduled
✅ **Realistic** - 1-2h transition/flex time

❌ **All work** - High Rupiah only
✅ **Balanced** - Mix of all categories, prioritize High Lifetime

❌ **Perfect adherence** - Guilt when miss
✅ **80% rule** - 80% adherence = excellent!

❌ **Rigid schedule** - Same time every day
✅ **Framework** - Adjust to daily reality

### Pro Tips

💡 **Start small** - Begin with 2-3 key blocks, expand later

💡 **Protect morning** - High Lifetime or High Rupiah only

💡 **Batch Low Rupiah** - Group admin tasks, don't scatter

💡 **Time-box Zero Rupiah** - If must do, limit strictly

💡 **Review weekly** - Compare actual vs Best Week

💡 **Celebrate wins** - Acknowledge good adherence

💡 **Learn from gaps** - Why did you diverge? Adjust template if pattern
