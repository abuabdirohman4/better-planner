## TROUBLESHOOTING COMMON PROBLEMS

### Problem: Habit completion declining over month

**Symptoms:**
- Week 1: 70%
- Week 2: 60%
- Week 3: 50%
- Week 4: 40%

**Solutions:**
1. **Fatigue/Burnout**
   - Reduce habit count
   - Lower monthly goals
   - Add rest days

2. **Lost motivation**
   - Review WHY (reconnect with purpose)
   - Celebrate wins so far
   - Adjust reward system

3. **Life circumstances changed**
   - Adjust habits to new reality
   - Pause non-essential habits
   - Focus on keystone habits

### Problem: Weekend drop-off

**Symptoms:**
- Weekday: 85%
- Weekend: 30%

**Solutions:**
1. Different weekend template
2. Accountability partner (weekend check-in)
3. Schedule specific times (not "whenever")
4. Link to enjoyable activities

### Problem: All-or-nothing thinking

**Symptoms:**
- Miss one day → stop trying
- One bad week → abandon month

**Solutions:**
1. Focus on percentage, not perfection
2. 70% is GOOD (not failure)
3. Every day is fresh start
4. Celebrate progress, not perfection

## USAGE TIPS

### Daily
- Mark habits at consistent time (evening review)
- Don't mark future dates
- Be honest (only helps you!)
- Add notes for context if needed

### Weekly
- Review patterns every Sunday
- Identify weak spots
- Celebrate wins
- Adjust next week's approach

### Monthly
- Do full review last day of month
- Export data for analysis
- Plan next month's habits
- Adjust goals based on reality
